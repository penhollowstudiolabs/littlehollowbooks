Let's Go, Intayon!

Avi & Kathleen Penhollow · Ages 0–5

A day in Ilocos Norte — with pandesal, jeepneys, music, and two languages moving together. From the majestic Padsan River to the lively Aurora Park, this dual-language picture book brings to life the scents of the bakery, the sounds of the street, and the sight of kalesas through the eyes of a young Filipino boy and his mother.

Rhythmic text based on an original song makes this a natural read-aloud. Ilocano and English on every page, equal partners.

Perfect for bedtime, classroom read-alouds, and any family keeping a language alive.
